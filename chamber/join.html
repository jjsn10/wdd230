Membership

Commerce Town offer different types of benefit according to the memberships.

Non-Profit

Price: Free

Benefits:

  • Add your business in our directory.
  • One free training events per month.
  • 15% off in all events per month.

Bronze

Price: $25 Monthly

Benefits:

  • Add your business in our directory.
  • Two free training events per month.
  • 25% off in all events per month.

Silver

Price: $50 Monthly

Benefits:

  • Add your business in our directory.
  • Three free ads per month in the main page.
  • 50% off in all events per month.

Gold

Price: $120 Monthly

Benefits:

  • Add your business in our directory.
  • Five free ads per month in the main page.
  • 75% off in all events per month.
