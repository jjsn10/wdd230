Audience

People from 25 to 40 years old who own a business and want belong to an association to promote and protect their commerce. These people should their businesses in Saratoga Spring City and they should be commited to promote the business activities and increase the leadership in this city.

Two Personas

John, an entrepreneur, who is in the range of age between 25 to 30 years old has opened a small business or startup and wants to innovate a product or service offered in Saratoga Spring.

Richard, an experienced businessman, who is in the range of age between 30 to 40 years old owns a consolidated business that provides high-quality services and products in Saratoga Springs.

Five Scenarios

John finds the chamber website useful to find partners to expand its operation and offer his services in Saratoga Springs.

John thinks the chamber website will help him to find companies to offer his service to improve their processes and be efficient in producing goods and service in the market.

Richard find useful the chamber website becasue He and his family always are locking for a new restaurant to eat something different close to their homes

John thinks that the chamber website will help to find Information technology companies who can offer IT products and services with his company to improve the business process of companies in Saratoga Springs.

Richard thinks that he can use the services offered by local Marketing companies to promote his services to help companies in Saratoga Springs to provide high-quality services.
