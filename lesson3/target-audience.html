Audience

People from 25 to 40 years old who own a business and want belong to an association to promote and protect their commerce. These people should their businesses in Saratoga Spring City and they should be commited to promote the business activities and increase the leadership in this city.

Two Personas

An entrepreneur from 25 to 30 years old who opened a small business or startup and wants to innovate a product or service offered in Saratoga Spring.

An experienced businessman from 30 to 40 years old who owns a consolidated business that provides high-quality services and products in Saratoga Springs.

Five Scenarios

An consolidated company with years in the market want to find partners to expand its operation in Saratoga Springs

An entrepreneur who is locking for offering her/his service to improve processes of consolidated companies with years of experiences in the market.

families locking for a new restaurant to eat something different close to their homes

Information technology companies who want to offer IT products to improve the services offered by companies in Saratoga Springs.

Marketing companies who want to offer marketing services to companies in Saratoga Springs to increase their revenues.
